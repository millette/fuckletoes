Fuckletoes (noun):

A person who is consistently clumsy or awkward, often tripping over their own feet or stumbling in various situations.
Someone who displays a notable lack of intelligence, making foolish decisions or repeatedly demonstrating incompetence.
An individual who is not only inept but also infuriatingly so, eliciting frustration or disdain from others due to their persistent shortcomings and inability to improve.
This definition encapsulates the playful, insulting, and saltier aspects of the term "fuckletoes," emphasizing both physical clumsiness and intellectual inadequacy in a manner that is both humorous and critical.
